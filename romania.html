Romanian Cuisine

Sarmale

Stuffed cabbage rolls filled with minced meat and rice.

Mămăligă

Traditional Romanian polenta, served with cheese and sour cream.

Ciorbă de burtă

A rich tripe soup with garlic, vinegar, and sour cream.

Mici

Grilled skinless sausages made from a mix of meats and spices.

Cozonac

Sweet bread filled with nuts, cocoa, or Turkish delight.

Papanasi

Fried doughnuts served with sour cream and fruit jam.
